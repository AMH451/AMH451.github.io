Inspiration

My projects, mainly the ones written in javascript, were inspired by many professional works. My Naruto platformer video game was inspired by classical
8-bit games such as Mario, Donkey-Kong, Space-Invaders, and Pac-Man. Most of my other projects were inspired by my teachers' examples and reference videos. My ultimate goal is to add more 8-bit games to this site since I chose the theme to be retro 80's video games. In the near future I will make a Pac-Man and Mario game.
